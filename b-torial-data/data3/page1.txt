<h1>Step 1: XDI Graph Model additional concepts</h1>

<p>Tutorials 1 and 2 covered the basics of the XDI Graph Model, including
the types of node and arcs, and some of the functions of context nodes, such
as entities, attributes, values, collections, and members. </p>

<p>In this Tutorial 3, we will explain the <span class="term">cloud number</span>, the <span class="term">cloud name</span>, <span class="term">equivalence</span>, 
the other two types of roots ( <span class="term">peer</span>, <span class="term">inner</span> ) and establishing the <span class="term">owner on a graph</span>.</p>
